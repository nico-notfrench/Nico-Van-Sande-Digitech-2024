Final Project - Christchurch Airport

Evaluation

Finally finished! It turned out just as I wanted, a replica of Christchurch Airport
that meets all the requirements I set, it is scaled, recognisable and detailed.
Anyone familiar with Christchurch Airport would quickly recognise it because of the
unique ATC tower, the terminal and Novotel. The users are myself and anyone else as
the final product is uploaded to SketchFab. People can use it to study or simply enjoy
for themselves. This model is entirely my intellectual property, created by myself
(Other than the trees which required no accreditation). In the end everything worked
out and along the way I learnt so many new functions and techniques to use in Fusion.
Receiving feedback throughout made sure that I came back to the detail instead of
expanding the project. I planned on working section by section ensuring that areas would
be completed before another gets started. With more time, I’d add further details like
windows, doors, pipes, or shipping containers.

I’m very proud of and happy with the final product. The process was challenging and
extremely time-consuming, requiring a lot amount of effort, but the result makes it
all worthwhile. Seeing the project as envisioned is very satisfying, and I quite
enjoyed every moment of creating it.
