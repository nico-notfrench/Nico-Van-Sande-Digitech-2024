Animation

Evaluation

The animation topic was quite unique. I enjoyed learning this side of
blender and how to use it to animate as I never thought this was possible.
I also liked fiddling around with the bones and how they control the character.

While it was interesting I dont think this is a great topic for my final
project. I didn't peak my interest as much as some other topics did and
doesn't seem to allow a massive amount of creativity.
